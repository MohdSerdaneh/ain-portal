Who am I?

Ain is an e-learning portal developed by Mohammed Abusirdaneh at UNM.
The platform was created to address a significant issue: the lack of accommodation for deaf and mute individuals in educational environments.
Ain provides a solution by recognizing American Sign Language (ASL) and interpreting emotions, enabling teachers to better understand and connect with their students.

Ain Portal

Final Year Project

  • Supports American Sign Language (ASL) recognition for real-time communication.
  • Detects emotions and facial expressions to understand student engagement.
  • Designed specifically for deaf and hard-of-hearing learners.
  • Bridges the gap between visual language and education.
  • Uses AI and deep learning for adaptive responses.
